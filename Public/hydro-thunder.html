Game of the Week

Hydro Thunder (2000)

"Hydro Thunder" is a high-speed boat racing video game developed by Midway Games. Originally released in arcades in 1999, it was later ported to various gaming consoles, including the Nintendo 64. In the game, players race high-powered speedboats through various exotic locations, navigating treacherous waters and performing stunts to gain boosts. With its fast-paced gameplay, colorful graphics, and exhilarating courses, "Hydro Thunder" became a popular title among racing game enthusiasts.
